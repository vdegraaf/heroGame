CHALLENGE YOUR VILLAN

a multiplayer game by Vincent de Graaf

GAME RULES

  1. Add player’s name
  2. Press ‘Start’ to see who may begin
  3. Every turn a player can choose one of the following options
    1. Pickup new weapon: a player can add a new weapon to his/her inventory. Hero's and Villan's have different options. Both have one default weapon
    2. Attack: click on your player in the Arena to attack your enemy with your Current Weapon (It is possible to iterate through your inventory to choose a different weapon if available.)
    3. Rest: click on your player rest-function to gain 5 health points
  4. When a player’s health hit 0 the other player has won the game. Click ‘Play Again’ to start a new game.
